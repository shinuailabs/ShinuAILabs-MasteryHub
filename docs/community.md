# 🤝 Community Guidelines

Welcome to **ShinuAILabs-MasteryHub** — a community-driven learning engine for Quality Engineers mastering AI.

## Our Ethos

- **Free & Open** — All content is free to use, fork, and share under the MIT License.
- **Respectful Collaboration** — Treat every member with respect. No discrimination, harassment, or spam.
- **Learn Together** — Ask questions, share insights, and help fellow QE professionals grow.

## How to Participate

- **⭐ Star the repo** — Show support and help others discover it.
- **🐛 Report Issues** — Found a broken link, typo, or outdated content? [Open an issue](https://github.com/shinuailabs/ShinuAILabs-MasteryHub/issues).
- **💡 Suggest Improvements** — Use the Issues tab with the `enhancement` label.
- **📢 Spread the Word** — Share with your QA/Testing network.

## Code of Conduct

### Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on learning and helping

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Spam or self-promotion
- Sharing proprietary/confidential content

## Content Standards

- All submissions must be original or properly attributed.
- Credit original authors when adapting content.
- No low-effort or AI-generated spam.

## Moderation

Maintainers reserve the right to remove content or block users who violate these guidelines.
