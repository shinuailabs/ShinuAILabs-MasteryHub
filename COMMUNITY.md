# 🤝 Community Guidelines — ShinuAILabs-MasteryHub

Welcome to the MasteryHub community! We're glad you're here.

---

## 🌱 Our Philosophy

MasteryHub is built on a simple belief: **Quality Engineering skills should be free and accessible to everyone.** We're a community of learners, builders, and testers who believe in:

- **Sharing knowledge openly** — Every Part is free, forever
- **Learning by doing** — 1,447+ hands-on labs, not just theory
- **Growing together** — Ask questions, share solutions, help others
- **AI for everyone** — Demystifying AI for every QA professional

---

## 💬 How to Participate

### 1. Learn & Practice
- Work through the Parts in order (each builds on the previous)
- Complete the projects at the end of each Part
- Use the cheatsheets for quick reference

### 2. Ask Questions
- 💡 **GitHub Discussions** — For questions, ideas, and conversations
- 🐛 **GitHub Issues** — For bugs, errors, or broken links
- Do a quick search first — your question may already be answered!

### 3. Share Your Work
- Built something cool from a project? Share it!
- Found a better way to do something? Open a PR!
- Created a LinkedIn post about your learning journey? Tag us!

### 4. Help Others
- Answer questions in Discussions
- Review pull requests
- Share tips and tricks you've discovered

---

## 📜 Code of Conduct

**Be respectful, be kind, be constructive.**

- 🚫 No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- 🚫 No spam or self-promotion
- ✅ Give constructive feedback
- ✅ Credit others' work appropriately
- ✅ Assume good intent

Violations may result in temporary or permanent ban from the community.
